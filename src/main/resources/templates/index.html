About us
Meet We are a company dedicated to animals, working with products for them and also with adoptions and purchases.Everything you need for your pet, whether wild or domestic, we have it here, we also work with orders if you can't find your order anywhere. We often adopt animals from the streets, those that the government cannot handle or also those rescued by third parties where they are available for adoption with a product bonus. We work with sales of several species such as armadillo, macaws, monk eys, insects among others that you can check in the catalog.
